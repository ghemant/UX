About the Project


SME Portal website is a salary disbursement and HR management solution for the Small and medium Enterprise (SME) corporates to cater their employees and clients.

My Roles and responsibilities

  • I was responsible for the SME portal's UX and UI design aspects, for which I proactively collaborated with Product Managers, UX researcher, Graphic designer, and developers across the team right from the onset of the project.

  • Conducted brainstorming sessions, taking key design decisions while creating the wireframes, presenting it to various stakeholders ( users / product managers / co-founders) and to deliver the final UI screens.
